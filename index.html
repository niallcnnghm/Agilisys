Pedro Mercedes Almodóvar Caballer; born 25 September 1949 is a Spanish film director, screenwriter, producer, and former actor.

His films are marked by melodrama, irreverent humour, bold colour, glossy décor, quotations from popular culture, and complex narratives.

Desire, passion, family, and identity are among Almodóvar's most prevalent subjects in his films.
